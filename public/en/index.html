People Detection

Our advanced AI can detect people in camera feeds with high accuracy. The system can track individuals, count people in a specific area, and alert when someone enters a restricted zone.

Applications:

  • Intrusion detection in secure areas
  • Crowd monitoring and management
  • Occupancy counting for safety regulations
Smart Feature:

Our digital guard post feature ensures at least one person is always present at a designated location, triggering alerts if the position is abandoned.

Vehicle Recognition

Pixelligence can identify and classify different types of vehicles including cars, motorcycles, trucks, and bicycles with high precision.

Vehicle Types Detected:

  • Cars and personal vehicles
  • Motorcycles and scooters
  • Trucks and commercial vehicles
  • Bicycles and alternative transport
Smart Feature:

Our smart counters can monitor parking lot occupancy in real-time, tracking entrances and exits across multiple cameras simultaneously.

Fire & Smoke Detection

Early detection of fire and smoke can prevent disasters. Our system can identify the first signs of fire or smoke and alert you immediately, potentially saving lives and property.

Key Benefits:

  • Early warning system for fire emergencies
  • Visual verification to reduce false alarms
  • Precise location information for faster response
Emergency Response:

When fire or smoke is detected, the system can automatically send alerts with video clips to emergency responders, giving them vital visual information before arrival.

Masked Individuals

The system can identify people with concealed faces, whether using masks, hats, or other items that might indicate suspicious behavior in secure environments.

Detection Capabilities:

  • Face masks and coverings
  • Hats and hoods concealing identity
Security Application:

Particularly valuable for banks, jewelry stores, and high-security facilities where concealing identity may indicate potential criminal intent. It can also be used to enforce mask usage or alarm in case of suspicious character appears.

Weapon Detection

Pixelligence can identify various weapons including guns, rifles, and knives in video feeds, providing critical early warnings in potential threat situations.

Detectable Weapons:

  • Handguns and pistols
  • Rifles and long guns
  • Knives and bladed weapons
  • Potentially dangerous tools in restricted areas
Critical Response:

When weapons are detected, Pixelligence can immediately alert security personnel with precise location information, enabling rapid response to potential threats.

Animal Recognition

Our system can identify various animals, with current focus on cats, dogs, and horses. This is useful for property protection, wildlife monitoring, or pet security.

Current Animal Detection:

  • Cats and felines
  • Dogs of various breeds and sizes
  • Horses
Animal Management:

Pixelligence can help manage issues caused by animals, such as detecting stray animals in restricted areas, monitoring livestock, or ensuring pet safety.

Continuous Learning System

Unlike traditional security systems, Pixelligence features an advanced self-learning capability that allows it to continuously improve its detection accuracy over time by adapting to your specific environment and camera setups.

Adaptive Learning Benefits:

  • Environment Adaptation: The system learns the specific visual characteristics of your cameras' locations
  • Lighting Adjustment: Automatically calibrates to different lighting conditions throughout the day and seasons
  • False Positive Reduction: Increasingly accurate at distinguishing between real threats and benign movements
  • Camera-Specific Optimization: Tunes detection parameters for each individual camera's position and angle
Performance Improvement:
The learning system ensures Pixelligence evolves with your organization, providing tailored security solutions that meet your specific needs.

Custom Training

For unique security challenges, we develop custom AI models tailored specifically to your organization's needs. Our team works with you to collect relevant data, engineer a specialized solution, and deploy a model that identifies exactly what you need to monitor.

Custom Model Development Process:

  • Data Collection: We gather data specific to your use case, either from your existing systems or through a controlled collection process
  • Model Training: Our AI experts build and train a unique model designed exclusively for your detection requirements
  • Testing & Refinement: The model undergoes extensive testing in your environment to ensure optimal performance
  • Deployment & Integration: The custom model is integrated with your Pixelligence system
